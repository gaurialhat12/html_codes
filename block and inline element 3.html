india

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area; the most populous country since 2023; and, since its independence in 1947, the world's most populous democracy.

India's uniqueness stems from its rich cultural heritage, including being the birthplace of major religions and home to ancient cities like Varanasi, its diverse geography from the Himalayas to tropical coasts, and its vibrant modern culture seen in its massive film industry, Bollywood, and the world's largest film industry, Bollywood, and its prominent place as the world's largest democracy.
